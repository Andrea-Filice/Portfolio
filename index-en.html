My name is Andrea Filice, a 17-year-old student. I develop video games with C# and Unity, websites with HTML and CSS, and JavaScript applications with Electron and NodeJS.

I started getting into computer science in first year of middle school, when I began asking myself "How do you create a video game?". I started with Construct 3 (an editor with block-based programming language) and after a few years, I moved on to Unity to expand my knowledge even further. From first year of high school, I began learning HTML and created some small websites as personal projects. In 2024, the official website of my video games went live. In third year of high school, I started working with Electron (a framework for creating applications through JavaScript code) and completed my first project for professional management of server maintenance services for companies.
My future projects are mainly 2: developing my knowledge with Unity from 2D to 3D, and creating a to-do app that automatically syncs across PC, Tablet, and Phone with AI integration, helping to organize the day.

Projects

All my projects, from video games to business applications.

Course Of Death

Course Of Death -Ultimate Chance- is a small battle royale with different maps to fight against bots and other players to win a special trophy... the crown!

Square Royale

Square Royale is a small Multiplayer and Singleplayer game where you must defeat all enemies across 12 different maps with various power-ups that can be purchased before starting the game.

Code Unlocker

Code Unlocker is a brain-storming game where the objective is to find the correct number combination by carefully observing the question the level requires. Each level is different and more difficult than the previous one, up to level 110, the last of Code Unlocker. If you complete all 110 levels and want to play again, be sure you'll earn unlimited gameplay, a reward with which you can choose the level and play it without limits!

Runners

Runners is a platformer game where the objective is to avoid falling to the ground and reach the end! The player's speed will increase as you progress through the map to make the game more difficult! If you fall before the end, you will receive rewards: experience points (to level up) and coins (to buy another skin for 10,000!). If you reach the end, congratulations, you won! You'll receive the same rewards, but in greater quantities!

Cube Invasion

Cube Invasion is a Tower Defense game, whose purpose is to buy with money (earned by killing the enemy cubes that try to attack you) all available towers! The towers will shoot at the nearest enemy and try to kill it, but you can help them by clicking on the enemy cube to take away its life!

Taskify Business

A simple application created with Electron for companies that allows managing and scheduling server maintenance.
